Me:

I am a neurodivergent genderfluid femme. I am 30 years old.

I am relatively active, though my day job is sedentary. I weigh about 150lbs (68kg) and am 5'5" (165cm). I eat a plant based diet consisting of lots of carbohydrates and vegetables. My diet is generally low in sugars. I consume alcohol occasionally; I don't use other substances. I grew up in Upstate New York and have spent the past 15 years in North Carolina.

I had two separate DVT (blood clots), both in my left leg, about a year apart before beginning HRT. I have been taking a prophylactic anticoagulant (5mg twice daily) since the second DVT. I have not had any DVT since, even with estradiol levels as high as 800 pg/mL.

I experience complex health including:

- hypersensitive nervous system & sensory processing disorder (stuff hurts and feels a lot)
- thrombophillia (blood clots easily)
- GI (tummy) dysfunction
- Anxiety, with a history of depression

Each of these (except the thrombophillia) have significantly improved since beginning estrogen :3
